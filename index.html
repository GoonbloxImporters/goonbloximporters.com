What Makes It Special

Packed With Systems

MergePets isn't just merging — it's an entire world of interconnected progression systems, all designed to be fun and fair.

🏠

Build Your Home

Choose from 5 home styles with 3 upgrade tiers each. Furnish with beds, fridges, jukeboxes, and collect daily bonuses from themed objects.

NEW

Daily Events & Weather

22 weather types rotate every 4 hours per zone. Global daily events boost CPS, merge coins, rarity odds, and more with dynamic VFX.

Combo System

Chain 3+ same-family merges for escalating multipliers. Hit combo 6+ for gem drops. Fire particles and celebration sounds fuel the rush.
